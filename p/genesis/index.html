About Us

The project was pioneered by Palash Chatterjee in May 2024, as a Summer Project. What was stared as a one person project with a simple AI image generation tool has now become a large project with multiple teams and a wide array of objectives.

Genesis is a project dedicated to pioneering the development of state-of-the-art artificial intelligence models and tools. Our mission extends beyond innovation; we aim to explore the practical applications and commercial viability of our advanced technologies.

The project has a major focus on

  • Innovative AI Development
  • Creative Product Solutions
  • Strategic Market Engagement

Divisions

Specialized teams for each step

  • Development
  • Production
  • Marketing

Development Division:

Focuses on creating advanced AI models and tools using cutting-edge technology. Ensures robustness and innovation in AI solutions.

The Development Division is the cornerstone of Genesis, focusing on the Production and refinement of advanced artificial intelligence models and tools. This division leverages cutting-edge technologies and methodologies to build robust and innovative AI solutions. The primary responsibilities include researching new AI techniques, developing state-of-the-art models, and ensuring the tools are reliable and effective. Collaboration with other divisions is essential to tailor these tools for practical applications and to drive continuous innovation.

Production

Uses developed and external tools to create practical, market-ready products. Specializes in turning AI capabilities into real-world applications.

The Production Division takes the AI models and tools developed by the Development Division and utilizes them, along with external tools, to create market-ready products. This division is responsible for experimenting with new ideas, developing prototypes, and ultimately producing commercial products that leverage AI capabilities. The Production Division focuses on practical applications, ensuring that the AI tools are used effectively to solve real-world problems and meet market demands.

Marketing

Promotes AI tools and products. Develops strategies to engage target audiences and differentiates Genesis’s offerings in the market.

The Marketing Division handles the promotion of the tools and products developed by Genesis. This division devises strategies to reach and engage target audiences, differentiating Genesis's offerings from competitors. Responsibilities include running marketing campaigns, gathering customer feedback, and driving brand awareness. The Marketing Division plays a crucial role in ensuring that the developed products gain traction in the market and align with customer needs and expectations.
